## What is FlagForge?

FlagForge lets you deploy code to production and control who sees it — without redeploying.

Wrap any feature in a flag, then control rollout from a dashboard:
- Roll out to 5% of users, watch your error rates, then increase to 100%
- Target specific users, tenants, or environments
- Kill switch any feature in seconds without a hotfix

Built for teams who want the power of LaunchDarkly without the $1,200/month bill.
